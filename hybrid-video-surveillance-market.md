<p><p>The Hybrid Video Surveillance Market encompasses two primary types: Analog Video Surveillance Systems and IP Video Surveillance Systems. Analog systems use traditional coaxial cables and are typically less expensive, making them suitable for basic applications. In contrast, IP Video Surveillance Systems leverage internet protocol technology, allowing for higher resolution images, remote access, and advanced analytics. The hybrid approach combines both systems, providing flexibility and scalability to meet diverse security needs, making it an attractive option for many organizations.</p></p>
