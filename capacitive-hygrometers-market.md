<p><strong>The Capacitive Hygrometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Relative Type</li><li>Absolute Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The capacitive hygrometers market is segmented into two types: relative and absolute hygrometers. Relative type measures humidity based on the changes in capacitance due to moisture content in the air, providing readings relative to the maximum moisture the air can hold. In contrast, absolute type gauges total moisture regardless of temperature or pressure, offering a direct measurement of water vapor concentration. Both types are crucial in various applications, including industrial processes, environmental monitoring, and HVAC systems.</p></p>
