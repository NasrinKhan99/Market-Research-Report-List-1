<p><p>The automobile lubricating oil market comprises several types. Traditional lubricating oils are derived from refining crude oil and are widely used for their performance and cost-effectiveness. Hydrogenated lubricants are produced through the hydrogenation process, enhancing stability and reducing impurities, which improves engine efficiency and longevity. Isomerization dewaxing lubricants involve altering the molecular structure of base oils to lower viscosity and improve fluidity at lower temperatures, making them suitable for extreme climate conditions and enhancing overall engine performance.</p></p>
