<p><strong>The Uncooled Thermal Infrared Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The uncooled thermal infrared detector market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Microbolometer IR Detectors** are sensitive to temperature changes and widely used in thermal imaging applications due to their high resolution and cost-effectiveness. </p><p>2. **Thermopile IR Detectors** operate based on the thermoelectric effect, measuring thermal radiation and commonly used in non-contact temperature measurement and gas detection.</p><p>3. **Pyroelectric IR Detectors** sense changes in infrared radiation, making them ideal for motion detection and security systems. Each type serves distinct applications across various industries.</p></p>
