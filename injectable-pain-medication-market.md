<p><p>The injectable pain medication market consists primarily of two key types: Nonsteroidal Anti-Inflammatory Drugs (NSAIDs) and central analgesics. NSAIDs, such as ketorolac, provide effective pain relief by reducing inflammation and blocking pain signals. They are commonly used for post-operative and acute pain management. Central analgesics, including opioids like morphine, work by acting on the central nervous system to alleviate severe pain. Both categories are crucial for various medical settings, addressing diverse pain management needs for patients.</p></p>
