<p><strong>The ECHO Cardiography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>M-mode</li><li>Two-dimensional (2-D, B-mode or real time)</li><li>Doppler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ECHO cardiography encompasses three primary market types: M-mode, 2-D, and Doppler. M-mode provides a single-dimensional view of cardiac structures over time, ideal for measuring chamber dimensions. Two-dimensional (2-D) or B-mode offers real-time imaging, allowing for comprehensive visualization of heart anatomy and function. Doppler ECHO evaluates blood flow dynamics, including velocity and direction, by analyzing frequency shifts in sound waves. These methods are essential in diagnosing and monitoring various cardiovascular diseases, enhancing clinical decision-making.</p></p>
