<p><p>The Hot Melt Polyurethane Adhesive market is divided into two main types: Non-Reactive and Reactive Hot Melt Adhesives. Non-Reactive Hot Melt Adhesives provide instant bonding, ideal for quick assembly and packaging applications, while Reactive Hot Melt Adhesives, which cure through a chemical reaction, offer enhanced durability and resistance to environmental factors. Reactive types are often used in demanding conditions, such as automotive and construction industries, whereas non-reactive types suit lower-stress applications like furniture and craft projects.</p></p>
