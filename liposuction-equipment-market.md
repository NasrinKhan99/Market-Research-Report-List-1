<p><p>The liposuction equipment market encompasses various technologies tailored for body contouring procedures. Negative-pressure liposuction equipment utilizes suction to remove fat, allowing for precise fat removal. Ultrasonic liposuction equipment employs high-frequency sound waves to emulsify fat before extraction, enhancing efficiency and minimizing trauma. Power-assisted liposuction equipment features a mechanized component that aids the surgeon in breaking down fat, reducing fatigue during lengthy procedures. Each type caters to different patient needs, surgeon preferences, and clinical scenarios, driving market diversity.</p></p>
