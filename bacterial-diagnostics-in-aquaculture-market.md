<p><p>Bacterial diagnostics in aquaculture encompass various techniques to identify pathogens affecting fish and shellfish health. Molecular diagnostics involve genetic analysis to detect bacterial DNA, providing precise identification. Immunofluorescent antibody tests utilize fluorescently labeled antibodies to visualize specific bacterial infections under a microscope. Polymerase chain reaction (PCR) amplifies DNA to detect even low concentrations of bacterial pathogens swiftly. Other methods include culturing techniques and biochemical tests, enhancing disease management and ensuring the health of aquaculture species effectively.</p></p>
