<p><ul><li>Horizontal Closed Tank</li><li>Silo/ Vertical Closed Tank</li><li>Open Tank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The milk storage tank market comprises three main types: horizontal closed tanks, silo/vertical closed tanks, and open tanks. Horizontal closed tanks are cylindrical and designed for efficient space utilization, providing insulation and temperature control. Silo or vertical closed tanks are tall and cylindrical, maximizing storage capacity and minimizing land use, ideal for large-scale dairies. Open tanks are uncovered, allowing for easy access and monitoring, typically used in smaller operations. Each type is selected based on specific storage needs and operational requirements.</p></p>
