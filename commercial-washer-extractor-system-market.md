<p><p>The commercial washer extractor system market is primarily categorized into open pocket and divided-cylinder systems. Open pocket washers feature large, accessible drums that allow for easy loading and unloading of bulk laundry. This design is efficient for handling large volumes of textiles. In contrast, divided-cylinder systems consist of multiple compartments within a single unit, enabling simultaneous washing of different types or colors of laundry. This versatility allows for better resource management and operational efficiency in commercial laundry settings.</p></p>
