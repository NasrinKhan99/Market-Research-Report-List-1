<p><p>RTD PT100 sensors are widely used for precise temperature measurement, categorized mainly into wire-wound and thin film types. Wire-wound RTDs consist of a fine wire coil wrapped around a ceramic or glass core, offering high accuracy and stability, ideal for laboratory settings. Thin film RTDs, on the other hand, utilize a thin layer of metal deposited on a substrate, providing faster response times and compact sizes, making them suitable for industrial applications where space and rapid readings are crucial.</p></p>
