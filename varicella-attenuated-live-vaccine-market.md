<p><p>The Varicella Attenuated Live Vaccine market includes two main types: monovalent vaccines and combination vaccines. In contrast, combination vaccines incorporate multiple antigens, often including those for measles, mumps, and rubella, alongside the varicella component. This approach enhances immunization efficiency by reducing the number of injections required for children, promoting adherence to vaccination schedules and improving overall public health outcomes related to these infectious diseases.</p></p>
