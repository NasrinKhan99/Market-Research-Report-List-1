<p><p>The electromechanical switch market comprises various types tailored for specific applications. Tactile switches provide user feedback through a noticeable click, while rotary switches allow for multiple positions through rotation. Encoder switches convert motion into electrical signals, and toggle switches operate by flipping a lever. Push switches activate circuits with a button press, whereas detect switches sense conditions to trigger actions. Micro switches are compact and reliable for low-power applications, and dip switches facilitate settings adjustments. Other markets include specialty switches for unique requirements.</p></p>
