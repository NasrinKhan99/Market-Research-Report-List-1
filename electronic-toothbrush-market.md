<p><strong>The Electronic Toothbrush Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vibration Motion</li><li>Rotation-Oscillation Motion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic toothbrush market comprises primarily two types of motion: vibration motion and rotation-oscillation motion. Vibration motion toothbrushes utilize sonic technology to create rapid bristle vibrations, effectively cleaning teeth and gums without the need for vigorous scrubbing. In contrast, rotation-oscillation toothbrushes feature bristles that rotate in one direction and then the opposite, delivering a thorough cleaning action. Both types aim to enhance oral hygiene through efficient plaque removal, but they cater to different preferences and cleaning techniques among users.</p></p>
