<p><ul><li>Dynamic Imaging</li><li>Static Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>X-ray detectors in medical imaging are categorized into dynamic and static imaging types. Dynamic imaging detectors, such as those used in fluoroscopy, capture real-time moving images, allowing for the observation of dynamic physiological processes. In contrast, static imaging detectors produce high-resolution still images, primarily utilized in conventional radiography and mammography for detailed anatomical examinations. Each type plays a crucial role in diagnosis and treatment planning, addressing different clinical needs within the medical imaging landscape.</p></p>
