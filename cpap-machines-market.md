<p><p>The CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure) machines market comprises two main types: Fixed Pressure CPAP Devices and Auto Adjusting CPAP Devices. Fixed Pressure CPAP Devices deliver a constant airflow pressure, tailored to the user’s needs, ensuring consistent treatment throughout the night. In contrast, Auto Adjusting CPAP Devices automatically adjust the pressure levels based on the user’s breathing patterns, providing a more personalized and comfortable experience. Both types are essential in managing sleep apnea and improving respiratory health.</p></p>
