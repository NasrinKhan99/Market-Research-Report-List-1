<p><p>Ophthalmology Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) are critical devices used in ocular surgeries to cut, coagulate, and manipulate tissues. The market includes two main types: Monopole Electrosurgical Units, which use a single electrode and return pad for current flow, ideal for precise cuts; and Bipolar Electrosurgical Units, employing two electrodes for localized coagulation, reducing thermal spread. Additionally, Vessel Sealing Units are specialized for sealing blood vessels effectively, minimizing blood loss during surgical procedures, making them essential for enhanced surgical outcomes.</p></p>
