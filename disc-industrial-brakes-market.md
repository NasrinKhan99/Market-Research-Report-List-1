<p><p>The disc industrial brakes market consists of two primary types: fixed disc type and floating plate type. Fixed disc brakes are mounted securely, providing consistent performance and effective heat dissipation, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, floating plate brakes allow for slight movement during operation, which helps accommodate misalignment and reduces wear. This flexibility enhances efficiency and lifespan, making floating plate types ideal for various dynamic industrial environments. Both types are essential for ensuring safety and reliability in industrial machinery.</p></p>
