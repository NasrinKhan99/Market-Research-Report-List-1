<p><ul><li>Continuous Feeding</li><li>Discontinuous Feeding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital cutting machine market is segmented into two main types: continuous feeding and discontinuous feeding. Continuous feeding machines allow the rolling or feeding of material in a seamless stream, enabling high-speed production and efficiency for large volumes. In contrast, discontinuous feeding machines handle individual sheets or pieces, offering flexibility for diverse materials and smaller batch sizes. Each type caters to specific manufacturing needs, balancing speed and versatility to optimize production processes in various industries.</p></p>
