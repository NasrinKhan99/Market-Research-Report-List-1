<p><p>Cladding systems equipment market encompasses two primary segments: interior cladding and exterior cladding. Interior cladding involves materials used to finish walls and ceilings within buildings, enhancing aesthetics, insulation, and soundproofing. It includes options like panels, tiles, and vinyl. Exterior cladding, on the other hand, comprises materials applied to the building's facade for protection against weather elements, improving energy efficiency, and providing an appealing exterior. Common materials include metal, wood, fiber cement, and brick, serving both functional and decorative purposes.</p></p>
