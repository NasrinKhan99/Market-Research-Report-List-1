<p><p>The dimming film market is segmented into two main types: self-adhesive and non-adhesive films. Self-adhesive dimming films can be easily applied to glass surfaces, offering flexibility and convenience for users who want to switch between transparent and opaque states. In contrast, non-adhesive films typically require additional installation methods, such as frameworks or mounting, making them suitable for permanent applications. Both types serve various sectors, including automotive, architectural, and consumer electronics, enhancing privacy, aesthetics, and energy efficiency.</p></p>
