<p><p>Gastroenterology Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) are essential devices used in minimally invasive procedures to cut, coagulate, or dissect tissue. The market includes three main types: Monopole Electrosurgical Units, which use a single active electrode for procedures; Bipolar Electrosurgical Units, which utilize two electrodes to pass current through the tissue, minimizing damage to surrounding areas; and Vessel Sealing Units, designed specifically to achieve hemostasis by sealing blood vessels. Each type caters to specific surgical needs and enhances procedural efficiency.</p></p>
