<p><ul><li>Mechanical Filter</li><li>Electrostatic Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disposable breathing filters market features two primary types: mechanical filters and electrostatic filters. Mechanical filters operate by physically trapping particles and pathogens in a mesh or fibrous material, ensuring clean air is inhaled. In contrast, electrostatic filters leverage static electricity to attract and capture airborne contaminants, enhancing filtration efficiency. Both types are crucial in applications such as healthcare, industrial settings, and personal protection, catering to the growing demand for improved respiratory safety in various environments.</p></p>
