<p><strong>The Nano-Cellulose Fibre Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cellulose Nano Fibrils (CNF)</li><li>Cellulose Nano Crystals (CNC)</li><li>Bacterial Nanocellulose (BNC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nano-cellulose fibre market comprises three main types: Cellulose Nano Fibrils (CNF), which are flexible and provide excellent mechanical properties, making them ideal for reinforcement in composites; Cellulose Nano Crystals (CNC), characterized by their high strength and rigidity, suitable for applications in coatings and films; and Bacterial Nanocellulose (BNC), produced by bacteria, offering superior purity and biocompatibility, often used in medical and food-related applications. Each type has distinct characteristics and applications across various industries.</p></p>
