<p><strong>The Traction Transformer of Locomotive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Alternative Current (AC) systems</li><li>Direct Current (DC) systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The traction transformer of locomotive market is primarily divided into two types: Alternating Current (AC) systems and Direct Current (DC) systems. AC systems utilize transformers to step down high voltage from overhead lines for efficient power distribution, enhancing propulsion and energy efficiency. In contrast, DC systems convert electrical energy directly from the supply lines to drive the locomotive, commonly used in urban and metropolitan transit. Both systems are essential for modern rail infrastructure, catering to different operational and environmental needs.</p></p>
