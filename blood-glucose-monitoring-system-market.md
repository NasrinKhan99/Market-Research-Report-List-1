<p><p>The Blood Glucose Monitoring System market comprises two primary types: Self-Monitoring Blood Glucose (SMBG) devices and Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) devices. SMBG devices allow individuals to check their blood glucose levels at specific intervals using handheld meters and test strips. In contrast, CGM devices provide real-time glucose readings through sensors placed under the skin, offering continuous tracking and alerts for fluctuations. Both systems are crucial for diabetes management, enabling users to make informed decisions about their health.</p></p>
