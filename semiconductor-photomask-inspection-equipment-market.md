<p><strong>The Semiconductor Photomask Inspection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor photomask inspection equipment market is divided into two main types: Die to Die (DD) Method and Die to Database (DB) Method. The DD Method compares an inspected die to another die from the same wafer, focusing on their relative similarities and differences. In contrast, the DB Method examines the inspected die against a comprehensive database of known good patterns, ensuring compliance with design specifications. Both methods play crucial roles in ensuring the quality and accuracy of semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
