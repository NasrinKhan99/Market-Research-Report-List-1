<p><p>Business Process Management (BPM) software is available in two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud BPM software is hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower initial costs, making it accessible for businesses seeking to manage processes without extensive IT infrastructure. In contrast, on-premise BPM software is installed locally on a company’s servers, providing greater control, customization, and security. Organizations choose between these options based on their specific needs, resources, and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
