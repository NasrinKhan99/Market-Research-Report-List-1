<p><p>The Military and Aerospace TVS (Transient Voltage Suppressor) diode market is categorized into uni-polar and bi-polar types. Uni-polar TVS diodes are designed to handle voltage transients in one direction, making them suitable for protecting sensitive components in systems with a defined positive or negative voltage. In contrast, bi-polar TVS diodes can manage transients in both directions, providing enhanced protection for applications where voltage may fluctuate in either direction. Each type is selected based on specific application requirements in military and aerospace environments.</p></p>
