<p><strong>The Healthcare Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary Packaging</li><li>Secondary Packaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The healthcare packaging market consists of two main types: primary and secondary packaging. Primary packaging directly contains and protects the product, such as vials, blister packs, and syringes, ensuring safety and maintaining integrity. Secondary packaging, on the other hand, involves the outer packaging that groups primary containers, including boxes and cartons, facilitating storage and distribution while providing additional branding and information. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring product safety, compliance, and effective supply chain management in the healthcare sector.</p></p>
