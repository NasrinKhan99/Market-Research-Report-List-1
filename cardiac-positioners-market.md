<p><p>The cardiac positioners market encompasses various types designed to optimize surgical access and patient safety. Negative pressure suction systems create a secure grip by applying suction, enhancing stability during procedures. Mechanically fixed positioners use clamps or supports to maintain positioning securely. Minimally invasive suction devices facilitate access while minimizing trauma to surrounding tissues. Apex suckers are specialized tools that adhere to specific anatomical points, allowing for customized positioning during cardiac surgeries, ultimately improving outcomes and efficiency in the operating room.</p></p>
