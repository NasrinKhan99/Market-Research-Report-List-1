<p><p>High-Performance Insulated Substrates for power modules primarily encompass two types: Aluminum Nitride (AlN) DBC substrates and Aluminum Oxide (Al2O3) DBC substrates. AlN substrates are known for their excellent thermal conductivity, enabling efficient heat dissipation, which is crucial for high-power applications. On the other hand, Al2O3 substrates offer good electrical insulation and moderate thermal conductivity, making them suitable for less demanding applications. Both substrate types are essential in enhancing the performance and reliability of power electronics in various industries.</p></p>
