<p><p>The 3D Digital X-Ray Systems market is primarily divided into two types: CR (Computed Radiography) and DR (Digital Radiography) tech systems. CR systems utilize imaging plates that capture X-ray data and are later processed digitally, offering flexibility and cost-effectiveness, especially in retrofitting older facilities. In contrast, DR systems employ digital detectors for real-time imaging, providing higher image quality and faster results, making them ideal for modern healthcare settings. Both technologies cater to diverse diagnostic needs and clinical workflows.</p></p>
