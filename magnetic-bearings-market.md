<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Magnetic Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Magnetic Bearing</li><li>Passive Magnetic Bearing</li><li>Hybrid Magnetic Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnetic bearings market is categorized into three key types: </p><p>1. **Active Magnetic Bearings (AMBs)** utilize electromagnetic forces to levitate and control rotating shafts, providing high precision and near-frictionless operation. </p><p>2. **Passive Magnetic Bearings (PMBs)** rely on permanent magnets for stabilization, offering simpler designs but limited control compared to AMBs. </p><p>3. **Hybrid Magnetic Bearings (HMBs)** combine elements of both active and passive systems, leveraging the benefits of each to achieve enhanced performance, efficiency, and reliability in various applications.</p></p>
