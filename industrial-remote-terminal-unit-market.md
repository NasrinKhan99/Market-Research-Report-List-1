<p><p>The Industrial Remote Terminal Unit (RTU) market consists of two primary types: wireless and wired RTUs. Wireless industrial RTUs leverage cellular, satellite, or radio technologies to transmit data over vast distances without physical connections, enhancing flexibility and installation ease. Conversely, wired industrial RTUs utilize fixed connections, such as Ethernet or serial communication, ensuring stable and reliable data transmission, ideal for environments requiring high security and consistent performance. Both types play crucial roles in monitoring and control across various industrial applications.</p></p>
